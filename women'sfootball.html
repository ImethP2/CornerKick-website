Welcome to the world of women's football.

Our website is dedicated to celebrating and promoting women's football. Here you will find articles, news, and information about female football players and teams from around the world.

Featured Players

Marta Vieira da Silva

Commonly known as simply Marta, she is a Brazilian forward who is widely regarded as the greatest women's football player of all time. She has won numerous awards, including six FIFA World Player of the Year awards and two Olympic silver medals.

Abby Wambach

An American forward who retired in 2015 as the leading all-time international scorer for men and women. She won two Olympic gold medals and a FIFA Women's World Cup during her career.

Christine Sinclair

A Canadian forward who holds the record for most international goals scored by a footballer, male or female. She has won two Olympic bronze medals and has competed in five FIFA Women's World Cups.
